Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

PrivateAI-Rewriter operates entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. No text ever leaves your device.

Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We use a third-party service, Amplitude, to collect anonymous usage data to help improve the app. This includes non-personal information such as:

  • Which features are used
  • How often the app is opened
  • Device type (e.g., iPhone, iPad)
  • App version

This data is collected without identifying you personally and is used solely to understand how the app is used and improve functionality.

Data Usage

All text rewriting occurs locally on your iPhone or iPad. The text you paste and the rewritten text are not sent off your device nor stored on your device.
